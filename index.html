ABOUT US

COMPANY ROOTS

Established on October 7, 2009, Jorbe Traffic & Hi-way Safety Systems Corporation is newly developed entity of Bermon Global Ventures which specializes in the safety requirements of our modern roads and highways.

With a noteworthy stature in the signage industry for two decades, Bermon Global Ventures Corporation has undertaken several road projects in the country, which includes:

  • Metro Manila Skyway Project Stage 1 for Hutama RSEA J.O. ⦾
  • South Luzon Expressway Rehabilitations for MTD Construction Phils., Inc ⦾
  • Subic-Clark Tarlac Expressway for Kajima Corporation ⦾
  • Cebu Coastal Road Project Tunnel Section ⦾
  • Cebu North Coastal Road Project Cansaga Bay Bridge for Ciriaco Corporation ⦾
  • Subic Access Road for Hanjin Heavy Industries and Corporation Co., Ltd ⦾
  • Northrail-Southrail Linkage Project (PNR) for Hanjin Heavy Industries and Construction Co., Ltd ⦾
  • Alabang viaduct for FF Cruz & Co. ⦾
  • South Luzon Expressway TR3 Project for Makati Development Corporation ⦾

OUR WORK

Anchored on the same commitment to quality, JORBE Traffic & Hi-ways Safety Systems Corporation is sufficiently equipped to be a key player in developing the country's urban environment by providing motorist and pedestrian clear directions, well-marked and secure roads, highways, airport runways and signages in accordance to international standards of traffic and safety management.

A member of Philippine Contractor's Association Board (PCAB) , JORBE Traffic & Hi-ways Systems Corporation delivers cost-effective and state of the art signage through its own group of highly qualified professionals with training on modern traffic and safety tecnology.

JORBE Traffic & Hi-ways Safety Systems Corporation is dedicated to meeting the needs of different clients nationwide with utmose regard on cost, quality, precision, and efficiency.
